The Unassailable "Why": Florida's Crisis is Our Opportunity

The data reveals a market in desperate need of a better solution. We are positioned to solve the specific, quantifiable struggles faced by thousands of families.

A Hidden Crisis

Of the 10.8% of Floridians 45+ with cognitive decline, a staggering number have not spoken to a healthcare professional.

Functional Difficulties

The majority report their memory problems have created real-world difficulties, interfering with daily life and work.

Immense Scale

50,000+

Individuals diagnosed with Alzheimer's in our 7-county service area alone, representing a massive, immediate market.

The Strategic "What": A Validated Blueprint for Care

We are implementing the **Florida Care Anchor Playbook™**, a meticulously designed framework that shifts the focus from simply treating the patient to supporting the entire family ecosystem.

Navigator Plan

For families needing expert guidance and coordination. We become their single point of contact, turning chaos into control.

Guardian Plan

Includes all Navigator services plus hands-on support from our dementia-trained aides to ensure safety and restore daily routines.

Anchor Plan

A fully bespoke, concierge case management service for complex cases, consolidating all care needs through one trusted partner.

Confronting the Challenges: A Strategy for Success

Our model is designed to turn the industry's greatest challenges—recruiting and certification—into our most powerful competitive advantages.

The Concern: A critical shortage of nurses and aides makes staffing difficult.
  • Our Mitigation: We become a premium, W-2 employer, offering stability and benefits.
  • Our Mitigation: We offer a genuine career path through our paid, mandatory **Anchor Care Curriculum™**.
  • Our Mitigation: Our high-quality, mission-driven model attracts top-tier clinicians seeking a better work environment.
The Concern: The Medicare certification process is long, complex, and financially risky.
  • Our Mitigation: Our phased GTM strategy de-risks the process by focusing on Private Pay first to generate immediate revenue.
  • Our Mitigation: We use the initial phase to build the flawless operational history and patient roster required by Medicare.
  • Our Mitigation: Our systems are built for "survey-ready" compliance from day one, smoothing the eventual application process.
